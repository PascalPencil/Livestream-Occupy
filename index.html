The Occupy movement is an international protest movement which is primarily directed against economic and social inequality. The movement proper started in Kuala Lumpur, with Occupy Dataran, followed by New York City’s Occupy Wall Street and Occupy San Francisco. As of November 17 the Meetup page "Occupy Together" listed Occupy communities in 2,609 towns and cities worldwide.
